World Happiness and its contributors

by Corné Heijnen (12230170)

data retrieved from Kaggle

This is a multi-linked view representing the happiness for all countries in the world. Hovering over the countries will reveal its overall happiness and its relative rank in the world. Moreover, the country's colors will be scaled according to the happiness. When a country is clicked, a pie chart for this country will be showed. This pie chart displays the contributions of a few standard contributors to the happiness score.

Part of the course DataProcessing at the University of Amsterdam
